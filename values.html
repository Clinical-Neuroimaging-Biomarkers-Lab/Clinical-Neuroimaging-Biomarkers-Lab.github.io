Our Values

The principles that guide our work

Scientific Precision

We pursue rigorous, reproducible research using advanced neuroimaging, computational modelling, and robust statistical methods to ensure our findings are reliable and meaningful.

Impact & Translation

We are committed to translating discoveries into real-world clinical applications, bridging the gap between neuroscience research and improved treatments for trauma-related disorders.

Ethics & Lived Experience

We centre the voices of those with lived experience in our research design and practice, ensuring our work is ethically grounded and meaningfully responsive to the communities we serve.

Open Collaboration

We champion open science, data sharing, and interdisciplinary partnerships, believing that transparent and collaborative research accelerates progress for everyone.

Innovation Through Adversity

We embrace creative problem-solving and novel technologies, from AI-enabled analysis to psychedelic-assisted therapies, to tackle the most challenging questions in mental health.

Excellence with Kindness

We foster a supportive, inclusive lab culture where high standards and compassion coexist, empowering every team member to thrive and do their best work.
